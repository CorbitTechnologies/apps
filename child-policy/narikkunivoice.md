## Safety Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

> We are committed to ensuring that our app is a safe environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any content, activity, or behavior related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

### Our Commitments

**1. Zero Tolerance Policy**
* We do not allow any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation within our app.
* Any attempt to share, promote, or engage in such activities will result in immediate suspension of accounts and reporting to relevant authorities.

**2. User Reporting & Moderation**
* Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly through our support channels.
* Reported content is reviewed promptly, and necessary action is taken.

**3. Compliance with Laws & Regulations**
* We comply with all applicable child protection laws and Google Play policies.
* Any violation is escalated to law enforcement when required.

**4. Preventive Measures**
* Our app is designed to prevent the upload or sharing of inappropriate content.
* We actively monitor and review reports to keep the platform safe.

**5. Education & Awareness**
* We encourage users to stay aware of online safety and report harmful content.
* Parents/guardians are advised to guide younger users in responsible usage of digital platforms.
