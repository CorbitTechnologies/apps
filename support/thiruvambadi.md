## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. How do I register as a business owner?
To list your business, navigate to the "Services" or "Shop" section and use the "+" button or contact us directly via the feedback form in the app menu.

### 2. How can I join the Blood Donor list?
You can update your profile information within the app and opt-in to the emergency blood donor directory by providing your blood group and matching contact details.

### 3. Is my data safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption through Google Firebase to ensure your personal data and contact information are stored securely. Please refer to our **Privacy Policy** for more details.
