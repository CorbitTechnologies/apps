## 2. Reporting Bugs

If you find a bug in the application, please provide the following details when contacting us:
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 13, Samsung S22).
- Operating system version (e.g., iOS 17, Android 14).
- A clear description of the issue.
- Steps to reproduce the bug.
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible).

---

## 3. Business & Service Registrations

If you are a local business owner or service provider and need help with your listing:
- Ensure you have submitted your details via the **Business Registration** screen.
- Note that all new listings require **Admin Approval** before appearing in the app. This usually takes 24-48 hours.
- If your listing is still not visible after 48 hours, please contact us with your registered business name and phone number.

## 4. Feature Requests

We love hearing from the Narikkuni community! If you have an idea that would make this app better for everyone:
- Send your suggestions to our support email.
- Join our community WhatsApp channel (link available in the app) to participate in discussions.

## 5. Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please do NOT report it publicly. Instead, send a detailed report to **security@corbittechnologies.com** so we can address it promptly.
