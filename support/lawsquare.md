## ðŸ’¡ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1. **I cannot log in to my account.**
   - Ensure your internet connection is stable.
   - Verify that the phone number entered is correct.
   - If you are not receiving the OTP, please wait for 60 seconds and try "Resend OTP".
   - If the issue persists, contact us at the email above.

### 2. **I purchased a course/test, but it is not showing up.**
   - Please go to the **Profile** section and pull down to refresh.
   - Click on the **"My Courses"** or **"My Tests"** section to see your active purchases.
   - If it still doesn't appear, please email us your transaction receipt and registered phone number.

### 3. **How do I access free content?**
   - On the Home screen, scroll down to the **"Free Content"** or **"Explore Resources"** section.
   - You can also navigate to the **Free Videos** tab from the main menu.

### 4. **Can I watch videos offline?**
   - Currently, an active internet connection is required to stream video lectures to ensure content security and updates.
